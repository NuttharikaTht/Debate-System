What is Debate?...

A "debat" or "debating" is a contest of visions by people with different opinions or with opposing sides. It is one of the activities that demonstrates a democratic political culture that helps people make better voting decisions during elections.

derivation!

In the past, you will find Debate dates back to the Greco-Roman period. ancient middle east and ancient India, where the subject of debate is inevitable is philosophy, belief, religion or politics, which are closely intertwined with human beings of any era.

Debate Rules

The same principle is 1. There are issues chosen by the judges. 2. There is a division of support and opposition. 3. Each speaker will be given time to seek support or arguments on the issue. and cannot attack the other speaker or deviate from the point. 4. The person who can find the best reason or evidence to support his speech will be the winner.
